How to Use These Resources

🖨️

Print & Bring

Download and print resources to bring to your GP, specialist, or pharmacist. Completed trackers give your doctor valuable data.

📱

Use Digitally

Keep resources on your phone or tablet for quick reference. Some patients fill in daily trackers digitally and email to their care team.

👨‍👩‍👧

Share with Family

Action plans and checklists are designed to be shared. Caregivers and family members benefit from knowing what to watch for.

⚕️

Personalise with Your Doctor

Templates like the Asthma Action Plan need to be completed with your healthcare provider to reflect your personal targets.

⚕️ Resource Disclaimer: All downloadable resources are for educational and self-management support purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice. Always discuss your health management plan with a qualified healthcare provider before making changes to your treatment.
